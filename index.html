The Wedding

The dress for the wedding is casual. The ceremony will be in a meadow on the ranch and the reception will be a BBQ in the barn so dress comfortable and be ready for a ho down! Just don’t forget that this is going to happen in May, on the Oregon coast, so dress accordingly!

We are excited to have Brandon’s Dad, Tom performing the ceremony and to have the kids standing up for us. We are looking forward to sharing this time with you all and making some wonderful memories!

Our Story

Cowgirl from Texas meets Beau Hunk from Oregon

Brandon and Tara met at a conference in St Louis, MO in 2011 while Brandon was living in Salem, OR and Tara was living in Orting, WA. After maintaining a long distance relationship for about a year, they decided it was time to make it a short distance relationship. In June 2012, Tara moved to Salem, OR where two families became one. Brandon adamantly insisted that he’d never get married again but eventually Tara’s sweet southern charm wore through his defenses and on February 14th, 2014 they got engaged!

We hope you can join us on our special day!

What to do

For those of you who can stay with us for Sunday, May 10th after the wedding, we’re planning an awesome day on the coast and we’d love you all to join us! There’ll be a low tide at 12:30 PM on Sunday so we’re planning on making a day camp on the beach and crabbing and clam digging during the tide. We’ll be cooking, cleaning and eating fresh delicious crab on the beach! We’re excited to show any and all a true Coastal experience!
